The fiat on-ramp lets you purchase cryptocurrency directly with a credit card, debit card, or bank transfer without leaving ELO Token Demo. It is powered by **Ramper** (ramper.xyz), a fiat-to-crypto gateway embedded directly in the app.

## Prerequisites

- A connected Web3 wallet (see [Wallet integration](/features/wallet))
- A credit card, debit card, or supported bank transfer method

## How to use the on-ramp

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect your wallet">
    Click **Connect Wallet** and approve the connection. The Ramper widget uses your connected wallet address as the destination for purchased crypto.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Navigate to /onramp">
    Go to `/onramp`. The page loads a Ramper iframe widget. The widget URL is fetched via the Moralis `fiat` plugin (`Moralis.Plugins.fiat.buy()`), which initializes the session and returns the embeddable widget URL.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select a cryptocurrency and amount">
    Inside the Ramper widget, choose the cryptocurrency you want to buy and enter the fiat amount. Ramper supports multiple currencies and payment methods depending on your region.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter payment details">
    Provide your credit/debit card or bank transfer details within the Ramper widget. Ramper handles all payment processing and KYC requirements directly.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm the purchase">
    Review the transaction summary in the widget and confirm the purchase. Ramper processes the payment and sends the purchased crypto to your connected wallet address.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Technical details

The on-ramp is implemented as a Moralis plugin integration in `src/components/Ramper.jsx`. The component:

1. Checks whether `Moralis.Plugins.fiat` is available on mount
2. Calls `Moralis.Plugins.fiat.buy()` with `disableTriggers: true` to fetch the widget URL
3. Renders the URL in a sandboxed `<iframe>` with permissions for `accelerometer`, `autoplay`, `camera`, `gyroscope`, and `payment`

<Note>
  The Ramper widget requires the Moralis fiat plugin to be enabled in your Moralis server settings. If the plugin is not configured, the iframe will not load.
</Note>

<Tip>
  Ramper supports buying crypto with fiat directly to a self-custody wallet, so funds go straight to your MetaMask or Rabby address rather than to a custodial exchange account.
</Tip>
